What can we learn from nature?

Nature is older, wiser, smarter, and more beautiful than us. Our planet is very senior to us, it has fostered around 3.8 billion years of life, and since animals, plants, and all organisms have evolved to meet their needs within the limits of the planet. In our planet's vast history, we've only taken less than mere moments. Unfortunately, however, we've developed destructive habits that are changing nature's equilibrium, the way it has always been. Only recently, we have begun to understand the consequences of our actions. But that is for another website.

Natures unifying patterns from the biomimicry toolbox

  • Nature uses only the energy it needs and relies on freely available energy
  • Nature recycles all materials.
  • Nature is resilient to disturbances.
  • Nature optimizes rather than maximizes.
  • Nature rewards cooperation.
  • Nature runs on information.
  • Nature uses chemistry and materials that are safe for living beings.
